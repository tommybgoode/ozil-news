Laughing all the way to the bank

By Tom Blackbourn

May 22, 2020

It was announced this week that Özil intends to see out the remaining year of his contract at Arsenal ahead of a rumoured move to Fenerbahçe. Great news for all of us Özil-heads. And at an eye-watering £350,000 a week, great news for our Özil as well! Naturally, Özil promptly arranged a photoshoot to celebrate. Our favourite Özil snap from the shoot captures Özil literally laughing his way to the bank - onboard the number 70 bus.

When asked why on such a wage he still opts to take to the bus to the bank Özil laughed "There is just too much money to fit in my car!"". Except in German. "Just joking", he added. "Obviously I get paid by bank tranfer. I just like to go to the bank once a week to look at it." We at ÖzilNews of course know the real reason - he takes the bus because he is very environmentally friendly, he's just too modest to talk about it.

How does Özil spend his money?

One might question how someone can spend £350,000 every week. For Özil there is no better way to spend his money than on the love of his life, his pet pug Balboa. It is rumoured that he has recently hired the coaching staff of Joachim Löw to teach Balboa to play football for £50,000 a week. Not that it shows. By all accounts progress is slow with Löw being quoted as saying, "We knew that training a dog to play football would be a challenge, but Balboa isn't exactly a natural footballing talent. In fact my staff tell me he has 4 left feet."

"I've heard of a cat in goal. But never a dog a centre-mid"
Joachim Löw - Germany Head Coach
