Özil has a Baby - but is it any good at football?

By Tom Blackbourn

May 11, 2020

A hearty congratulations to Mezut and his wife Amine on the birth of their new baby girl from all of us here at ÖzilNews.de. Another Özil in the family and the potential for a whole new generation of Özil news. But a key question remains to be answered - is the baby any good at football?

Nature vs Nurture

It's a question as old as time itself. Is nature or nurture more likely to influence the development of a beautiful premier-league footballer's baby into a footy pro in their own right? To answer this, we have to put our faith in science. And what better way to answer the question than to find sets of twins (or even better triplets) that are up for adoption and separate them at birth without their knowledge. Unfortunately, we weren't in a position to do that here; it would have taken far too long. Fortunately for us we only have to look to a secretive study from the 1960s in which psychiatrist Peter B. Neubauer began a multi-year 'twin study' to observe the development of twins forcibly separated at birth, with the aim of making a Netflix documentary about it 60 years later.

Whilst the study itself was never completed the Netflix documentary aired successfully in early 2020. And anyone who has seen it, scientist or not, must surely take the same thing away from it - Nature plays by far the biggest role. All three of the separated triplets looked identical! And they all did wrestling and liked cars.

It sure sounds like before long we'll have a whole new load of Özil news to report on. But nature or nurture - we're sure the baby Özil will have plenty of both.
