Meet the Team

Fun

Tom Blackbourn

Editor in Chief

Tom Blackbourn is the Editor in Chief of ÖzilNews.de. A veteran sports journalist and long time Özil fan he is passionate about bringing you the latest Özil news from around the globe.

Playful

Jamie Waddell

Chief OÖzilzil Correspondent

Jamie Waddell is Chief Özil Correspondent at ÖzilNews.de. Jamie's passion for Mesut Özil was sparked when Özil famously rescued a tourist group in Germany's Black Forest from a pack of aggressive wild boars using his incredible footwork. Jamie was amongst those saved that day. He now dedicates his professional life to chronicling the life and times of Özil for the public.

Artsy

Alix Carpenter

Creative Director

Alix Carpenter is the site's Creative Director, regularly drawing inspiration from Mesut Özil's own all-round creative brilliance. Beyond his undisputed in-game flair, Alix believes that Özil's uniquely large-eyed aesthetic and often underrated rap talent make him not just a footballing legend, but a cultural icon of the 21st century.

Enthusiastic

Chris Laycock

Very Junior Reporter

Our most junior reporter, what Chris lacks in journalistic experience, he makes up for with a love of all things Özil. The fact that Chris’s extreme investigative journalism has led to Özil issuing a restraining order has not deterred Chris, who continues to report from afar.

Sneaky

Tom Muller

Shady Paparazzo

Muller has dedicated his life to capturing candid and voyeuristic images of Özil's legendary exploits. Equally adept at hiding in bushes waiting for that ideal snap as he is at using telescopic lenses to capture Özil at his most intimate moments, he is a true master of his exploitative and disreputable craft. When it comes to getting the perfect photo of Özil, there is no line that Muller is unwilling to cross.

Sturdy

David Sanderson

Head of Memes

A recent addition to the team, Dave loves all things pop culture. He has dedicated his life to the field of Memetics, firstly studying ‘Memes and the language of the Internet' at Newcastle University before completing his PhD in ‘The History and Culture of Memes' at Oxford University, where he wrote his highly influential thesis ‘The Meme-ing of Life’. Dave has since forged a successful career working for companies such as 4chan, Reddit and Imgur. Whilst meme culture continues to have its detractors in the journalistic community, his knowledge and extensive meme portfolio is immensely valuable to the ÖzilNews team in this digital age.

Wise

Jon Harris

Insights (Özil Affairs)

Since being born at a very early age, Jon Harris has dedicated his life to unravelling the mystery that is Mesut Özil. 'What is his favourite farmyard animal?'. 'Is he a fan of sea shanties?'.'How many steps (on average) does he take during the month of October?'. These are some of the mysteries that Jon has helped to solve, leading him to winning numerous awards, including the coveted 2018 'Mesut: The Man Behind the Eyes' award..
